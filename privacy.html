Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Porter's Nails & Spa ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect when you visit our website or book an appointment, how we use it, and your choices regarding text message and email communications.

Information We Collect

When you book an appointment online, by phone, or in person, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Phone number
  • Email address (if provided)
  • Appointment details (service requested, date, time, preferred nail tech, and any notes you provide)

We do not collect payment information through our website. Payment is handled in person at the salon.

How We Use Your Information

  • To schedule, confirm, reschedule, or cancel your appointment
  • To send you appointment confirmations, reminders, and updates by text message and/or email
  • To respond to questions or requests you send us
  • To improve our services and website

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Text Messaging (SMS)

If you provide your phone number when booking an appointment, you consent to receive text messages from Porter's Nails & Spa related to that appointment — including confirmations, status updates, reminders, and responses to rescheduling or cancellation requests.

Message frequency varies based on your appointment activity. Message and data rates may apply.

To opt out of text messages at any time, reply STOP to any message you receive from us, or simply call us at (281) 747-7421 and let us know. We will not text you further once you opt out.

Text messaging consent is never sold, shared, or used for marketing purposes, and is not shared with third parties for their own marketing.

For help, reply HELP to any message, or contact us directly using the information below.

Information Sharing

We use trusted third-party services to operate our booking system, including services for SMS delivery, email delivery, and secure data storage. These providers only receive the information necessary to deliver your appointment communications and do not use your data for their own purposes.

Data Retention

We retain appointment and contact information for as long as needed to provide our services and maintain accurate business records. You may request that we delete your information by contacting us directly.

Your Choices

  • You may opt out of text messages at any time by replying STOP
  • You may request a copy of the information we have about you
  • You may request that we correct or delete your information

Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without parental involvement in the booking process.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
